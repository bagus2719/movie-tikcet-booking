Synopsis

The Lion King is a classic Disney animated film that tells the story of Simba, a young lion destined to become king. The story begins with Simba's birth, celebrated by all the animals of the Pride Lands. However, this happiness is short-lived. Simba's ambitious uncle, Scar, plots to take the throne by tricking Simba, resulting in the tragic death of Simba's father, Mufasa.
